About GetOut

GetOut is your smart companion for organizing outdoor gear, planning adventures, and staying prepared on the trail or on the water.

Whether you're hiking remote mountain paths or paddling down wild rivers, GetOut helps you pack light, stay safe, and enjoy the experience. Inspired by the practical wisdom of experienced explorers, this app provides tools, checklists, and packing advice you can trust.

Built with love using Angular and Bootstrap, GetOut is lightweight, mobile-friendly, and focused on what matters most: helping you spend more time out there.

Privacy Policy

1. General Information

GetOut is a web-based application that communicates with a backend service (Spring Boot) and stores user data in a PostgreSQL database. This privacy policy informs you about the type, scope, and purpose of processing personal data within the app.

2. What Data We Collect

When using GetOut, the following personal data may be collected and stored:

  • Account data (e.g., name, email address, hashed password)

All data is stored securely on the backend server.
